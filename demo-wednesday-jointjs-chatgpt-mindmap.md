We've published a new demo: [JointJS+ ChatGPT MindMap](https://www.jointjs.com/demos/chatgpt-mindmap)

Over the past few months, we've probably all come across ChatGPT, which is rocking the (not just tech) world. It allows you to solve a range of complex tasks in the blink of an eye, either on its own or in combination with other technology. This Wednesday, we decided to introduce a possible combination of ChatGPT's capabilities and our professional library, JointJS+.
