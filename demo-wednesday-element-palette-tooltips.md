**We've published a new demo:** [**Element Palette Tooltips**](https://www.jointjs.com/demos/element-palette-tooltips)

Element Palette (also known as Stencil) has limited real estate. Therefore, you might wish to keep it compact and free of unnecessary information. Yet, your users may still want detailed insights into the elements. So, how can you balance both objectives: keeping users informed while maintaining a clean design?

Today's demo highlights the use of a tooltip plugin that offers users a detailed view of elements upon hovering. This approach elegantly provides more information only when needed. To see it in action, hover over any element in the Element Palette below and experience it for yourself!
