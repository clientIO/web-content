We've published a new demo: Spreadsheet Shapes with Handsontable

In our latest demo application, we showcase how we've integrated the Handsontable third-party spreadsheet library with JointJS. This demonstration includes the insertion of a table into a JointJS shape using a custom element view, aligning ports to the center of the columns, and introducing the concept of mapping with transformation functions between two separate tables.

Open the demo on CodePen and study its source code to learn more!
