## What is SOC 2 Type II Compliance?

SOC 2 (System and Organization Controls 2) is a voluntary compliance standard developed by the American Institute of CPAs (AICPA). The primary focus? Establishing and evaluating internal controls across five key areas:

- Security
- Availability
- Processing Integrity
- Confidentiality
- Privacy

SOC 2 Type II compliance specifically evaluates how effectively these controls are implemented and maintained over a sustained period, typically one year. **This rigorous assessment provides assurance that JointJS adheres to the highest security best practices on an ongoing basis.**
